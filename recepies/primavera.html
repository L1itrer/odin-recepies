Primavera

Description

A relatively quick and simple pasta dish without any meat. Preparation time is about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 spoons of olive oil
  • A handful of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 teaspool of oregano
  • Chopped basil
  • About 30 grams of grated parmesan cheese
  • 100-120 grams of your favourite pasta
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta using the instructions on the package. In the meanwhile let's prepare the rest of the dish.
  2. Peel the carrot and then keep "peeling" using the same tool you used to peel the carrot. You should achieve long stripes.
  3. Do the same with the courgette.
  4. On a pan heat up the oil. Add finely chopped onion and let it fry for a moment. Add the carrot and keep frying for 2-3 minutes. Add grated garlic at the end.
  5. Cut all tomatoes in half and add to the pan. Also add some salt, pepper and oregano. Cook for about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the courgette and cook until it gets soft (or for 3 minutes).
  7. Add strained pasta, mix all the ingredients on the pan and heat everything up for a moment.
  8. At the very end add chives, cheese and some more olive oil if you wish.
