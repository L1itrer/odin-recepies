Broad bean salad

Description

A decent summer salat with broad bean as the main ingredient. Good with meat and/or grill.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of broad beans
  • About 6 medium-sized potatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • 250 grams of bacon

Sauce ingredients

  • 300-400 grams of natual yoghurt
  • Mayo
  • 2 spoons of spicy mustard

Steps

  1. Cook the broad bean in about 1 liter of water. Dissolve some salt and suger in hot water before adding the beans. The beans should be cooked under cover for about 8 minutes depending on how young the beans are.
  2. Cook the potatoes in salted water without peeling them. Once they're soft strain them and peel. Cut them into small pieces and grill. You can use a grilling pan of normal frying pan if you don't have one. You can also skip grilling them if you're in a hurry.
  3. Cook the eggs in salted water until they're hard-boiled.
  4. Cut the bacon into small pieces. Fry it or cook it inside an oven.
  5. Peel all broad been and put them on a serving platter as the base of the salad. On top of the beans add a layer of grilled potatoes. Cut the eggs into small pieces and add them on top as well. Finally cover the eggs in bacon.
  6. Prepare the sauce. In a bowl mix all ingredients until they're one substance.
  7. Cover the salad in sauce. Chop the chives and sprinkle it on top of the sauce. The salad is finished
